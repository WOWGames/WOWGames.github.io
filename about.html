More About WOW Games

Hello! We are students at the University of California, Irvine. Our goal for this quarter in our Computer Development Game class is to create and develop an awesome, non-"match-three" game. However, we don't just make any game, we are also doing much more, like marketing and building hype and maintaining documentation -- almost as though we were a real game company.

We decided to go with a parody of a classic RPG (role-playing game). Although we have only ten total weeks to put everything together, we've been at this nonstop!

Don't forget to see our game, Milk! Check it out on the Games page!
